Percentage of Variance Explained

By checking the percentage of variance explained we can understand how much of the between sum of squares variance is explained relative to the total sum of squares at the different cluster values. Most likely we would want to add a screeplot and use the `Elbow Method` to consider the percentage of variance explained at different numbers of clusters all at once. Will be included in future work.
